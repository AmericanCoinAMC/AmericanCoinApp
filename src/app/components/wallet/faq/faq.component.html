Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create my AmericanCoin Wallet?

To create a wallet simply click on "Create Wallet". You will be asked to to follow several steps.

  • Enter a Password (Used to encrypt your Wallet File)
  • Download Wallet File (Used as a way of accessing your Wallet)
  • Print Paper Wallet
Following these steps will be ensuring your AmericanCoin Tokens safety.

How do I Save/Backup my Wallet?

You can Backup your wallet by downloading the Wallet File and/or Paper Wallet when creating your new wallet.

If you already own a Wallet and didn't download these files you can access them from your Wallet Dashboard.

*Hint: always keep these files on offline storage devices, such as USB sticks.

How do I Access my new Wallet?

You can access your new Wallet by using your Wallet File and your Password or you can simply use your Private Key

How do I send & receive AmericanCoin Tokens?

You can send & receive funds by Accessing your AmericanCoin Wallet. Always make sure you're browsing on ("https://www.americancoin.org/wallet").

Where are my AmericanCoin Tokens stored?

By creating an AmericanCoin Wallet you're implementing a very popular security technique called Cold Storage.

We do not store any information from any user in order to protect everyone's privacy.

The only access there is to a Wallet is through a Wallet File + Password and a Private Key. This is way we encourage AmericanCoin users to keep these pieces of information in safe places.
